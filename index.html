Here is a time line of Tesla's life:

  • 1856 - Nikola Tesla is born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia).
  • 1861 - attended the "Lower" or "Primary" School in Smiljan, where he studied German, arithmetic, and religion.
  • 1862 - The Tesla family moves to GospiÄ‡, Austrian Empire, where Tesla's father worked as a pastor.
  • 1873 - contracts cholera and is bedridden for nine months and near death multiple times.
  • 1875 - enrolls at Austrian Polytechnic in Graz, Austria
  • 1878 - severs all relations to his family to hide the fact that he had dropped out of school
  • 1879 - Tesla's father passes away.
  • 1881 - moves to Budapest to work as the chief electricain at the Budapest Telephone Exchange
  • 1882 - move to France
  • 1884 - moves to New York City in the United States where he is hired by Thomas Edison
  • 1887 - forms the Tesla Electric Company with the help of two other men.
  • 1887 - develops an induction motor running on alternating current (AC).
  • 1891 - at the age of 35, becomes a naturalized citizen of the United States.
  • 1899 - moves to Colorado Springs
  • 1943 - dies at the age of 86
